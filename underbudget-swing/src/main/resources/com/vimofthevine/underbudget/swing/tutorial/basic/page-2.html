Defining Estimates

Get started by creating a new budget (File->New). Once you've got a budget, you have to create your estimated incomes and expenses.

An estimate is anything that is expected to occur within the date range of the budget. There are three types of estimates:

  • Incomes : money earned (e.g., paychecks)
  • Expenses : money spent (e.g., groceries)
  • Transfers : transfers between accounts (e.g., credit card payments)
